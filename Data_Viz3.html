Data Analysis: Wind Speed vs. Latitude

This plot compares Wind Speed in MPH vs. Latitude. What stands out here is how remarkably calm it is. The vast majority of Wind Speeds under 20 MPH all over the world. Additionally only one data point above 40 MPH. This is a snapshot of one day admittedly, and certainly there are days with more data points above the 40 MPH threshold, but still amazingly calm.
